The Ramblings of Yaakov Shore.

The name is based on a verse in Psalms 110, Dew of your Youth is the blog of Yaakov Shore. According to the midrash (Rabbinic exposition on the Bible) this verse is referring to Abraham - who in his youth went against the zeitgeist of his times and rejected idolatry and spreading monotheism.

I've taken this verse as a symbolic homage to iconoclasts and free thinkers, who neglect the dogma of their time and follow their own beliefs and convictions.

The hebrew phrase טל ילדתיך is also the numeric value of my Hebrew name יעקב אפרים.

Yaakov Shore is a web developer, banjo enthusiast, husband and father based in Israel.

Check me out on CodePen, LinkedIn or GitHub.
